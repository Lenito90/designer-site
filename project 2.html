EduCompass Foundation

Logo & Website

Role: Designer

Client name: Voccess Trainings

The Edu Compass Foundation helps young people uncover and realize their potential to create a better present and future. It supports educators and educational institutions to develop and expand their capacity to provide adequate, innovative state-of-art education.

Branding

Logo

The Edu Compass foundation stands for radical innovation, originality and reform in the field of education. In its early days, it had adopted a rather conservation approach towards its branding - read royal blue Garamond style typography and many librarian pictures. However, those did not visually represent the change it aimed to bring in the educational world and we set off on a mission, starting with the logo and slogan, to make its style contemporary and elegant and its voice gentle but calling. We fulfilled this mission by opting for vibrant colours, sans serif typography, carefully considered pictures and minimalistic website layout.

Website

Intro

The foundation's website serves as a gathering of all project related information - it needs to be quickly and easily accessible to both to visitors and potential investors/collaborators.
